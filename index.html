🌴 Maldives Academic Mobility Programme 2026

A 14-Day Blended (Virtual + On-Site) Research, Teaching & Cultural Immersion Experience

In conjunction with ICSRI 2026 · 08–09 August 2026 · Villa College, QI Campus, Malé

Welcome to the Programme

Villa College — the first private college in the Maldives, established in 2007 — invites a delegation of 15–30 PhD research scholars from a partner Indian university to take part in a 14-day blended academic mobility programme. The programme is structured as 7 days of online pre-departure preparation followed by 7 days of on-site immersion in the Maldives, anchored around the prestigious 10th International Conference on Social Research & Innovation (ICSRI 2026), themed "Tides of Transformation: Shaping Sustainable Futures." A final block of 4 days of post-mobility consolidation activities at the home university rounds off the programme, ensuring scholars translate their experience into tangible academic outcomes.

Scholars will be culturally and logistically prepared through virtual sessions before departure, immersed in Maldivian heritage and hospitality on arrival, present at ICSRI 2026 (conference registration fee fully waived for mobility participants), engage in specialised research and publication skills workshops with access to top-cited researchers, and experience the islands through curated city tours, cultural performances, and a local island excursion. On return, scholars consolidate their learning through structured reflection and dissemination activities at their home university.
